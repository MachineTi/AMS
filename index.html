Welcome to AMS

Aftermarket sales was established 1986 by Heyns du Plessis, who has been in the auto parts business for many years. He started importation of general auto replacement parts, including Alfa Romeo and Jeep spares which he has been supplying for many years. He has since expanded into the 4x4 market by securing sole imports/distribution of Procomp suspension products. Other lines imported include Sheriff Skid Plates, US Wheels, Rock Krawler Suspensions and Nekarth Kaiser Lockers. Other imports from Europe include lines such as water pumps, brake discs, cylinders and kits, engine parts, gaskets, rubber components, suspension parts and restoration parts for older model Alfa Romeo & Jeep vehicles. We also import replacement parts for Fiat and French cars.

We specialize in:
  • European manufactured spares and replacement parts for most makes of motor vehicles
  • Explorer Procomp 4X4 suspension systems and accessories (sole importer)
  • Bestop 4X4 accessories (sole importer)
  • US Wheels (sole importer)
  • Jeep® and Alfa Romeo replacement parts
  • Procomp Wheels

Aftermarket Sales products are provided through a network of dealers in South Africa, Namibia and Botswana.
