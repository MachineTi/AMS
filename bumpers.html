AMS Bumpers

AMS Aftermarket Sales now offer their own line of Jeep Wrangler bumpers made to their specification!

The JK Wrangler front bumper - manufactured from 4mm steel with built in reinforced 3mm steel winch plate, and powder coat finish. It comes with separate front side-sections with the option to be bolted on, and include 2 HID spot lights. Also included are two strong recovery hooks and a nudge bar.

The rear bumper, in the same material and finish as the front, come standard with an optional swing out oversize tyre carrier bolted to the bumper. There is provision for a tow hitch. A complete rear bumper/swing out unit is available as well.
